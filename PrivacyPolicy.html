General Privacy Practices

The following practices apply to all Purus applications unless otherwise noted in app-specific sections below.

Information Collection and Use

User-Provided Information

Purus apps may acquire information you supply when you download, register, or use the application. Registration is not mandatory for most features, but some functionality may require it.

The Service Provider may use this information to contact you with important notices when necessary.

Automatically Collected Information

Purus apps may automatically collect:

  • Device type and model
  • Unique device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Operating system version
  • App usage patterns and behavior

Location Information

Purus apps do not collect precise real-time location data unless explicitly required for app functionality. When location data is collected, it is used for:

  • Core app features that require location
  • Personalized content where applicable
  • Analytics and performance improvements (anonymized)

Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Some Purus apps may use AI technologies for:

  • Feature recommendations
  • Automated functionalities
  • User experience improvements

All AI processing complies with applicable laws and this Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Access

Only aggregated and anonymized data may be shared with third parties. Information may be disclosed:

  • When required by law
  • To protect rights, safety, or investigate fraud
  • With trusted service providers under strict confidentiality

App-Specific Privacy Information

Purus.Health

Purus.Health is a digital health and medical record application for humans and pets.

Sensitive Data Notice: Purus.Health handles sensitive personal and medical data. This data is treated with the highest level of care and security.

Additional data collected by Purus.Health may include:

  • Personal health information and medical records
  • Medication and treatment history
  • Health measurements and vitals
  • Pet health records (if applicable)
  • Emergency contact information

Special protections for health data:

  • Health data is stored locally on your device by default
  • Cloud synchronization (if enabled) uses end-to-end encryption
  • Medical data is never shared with third parties for marketing purposes
  • You maintain full control over your health data export and deletion

Purus.Drive

Purus.Drive is a driving and vehicle management application.

Additional data collected by Purus.Drive may include:

  • Trip information and driving routes
  • Vehicle information and maintenance records
  • Fuel consumption data
  • Location data during active trips (with user permission)

Location data usage:

  • Location is only collected during active trip recording
  • Trip data can be stored locally or synced to cloud (user choice)
  • Location history can be deleted at any time

Opt-Out Rights

You may stop all data collection by uninstalling the application using standard uninstall methods on your device.

Data Retention

  • User-provided data is retained while you use the application
  • Automatically collected analytics data is retained for up to 24 months
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time

To request data deletion, contact: furfarch@me.com

Children's Privacy

Purus apps do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Users must be at least 16 years old to consent (or have parental consent where required by law).

Security

The Service Provider uses physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect your information. Sensitive data (especially health data in Purus.Health) receives additional encryption and security measures. However, no system can guarantee complete security.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of any Purus application indicates acceptance of changes.
